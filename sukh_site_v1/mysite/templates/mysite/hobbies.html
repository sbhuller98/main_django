Meet Baj

11 months

The best pandemic puppy

Baj is a German and King Sheperd cross. He's getting close to a year old now, so his days as a puppy are limited. He has been a great addition to the family. He gets about 2 hours of walks everyday, and in turn keeps all of us active. Even at home he's quite crazy and doesn't let you sit still for too long. I'm excited to take him along on my hikes and trips this summer. Baj isn't really a summer dog, his thick fur means he struggles to stay cool so we'll have to be vigilant and make sure he doesn't get sick during our adventures.

Great Outdoors

Exploring what BC has to offer

I've always enjoyed my hikes. They were a good way to stay fit and reward yourself with a nice view. This year, it became almost a passtime. With covid restrictions limiting what I could do and making it a struggle to stay in shape, I decided to make hiking a weekly thing. This summer I did a new hike almost every week and finshed the season by climbing the beautiful Panorama Ridge in Garibaldi Provincial Park. A need for speed

I got my first bike when I was 18, as soon as I was old enough. After that, I never looked back. Motorcycles are a cheap way to enjoy the extreme performance provided by todays vehicles. Also, there's the added thrill of being on two wheels and exposed to the elements. My favourite bike was my black GSXR-600 (top left picture), I still regret selling it. That being said, as I've aged I've come to accept the dangers that come with riding and it will likely be something I enjoy only for the next couple of years.

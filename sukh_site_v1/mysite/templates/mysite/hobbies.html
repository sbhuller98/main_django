The best pandemic puppy

Baj is a German and King Sheperd cross. He's getting close to a year old now, so his days as a puppy are limited. He has been a great addition to the family. He gets about 2 hours of walks everyday, and in turn keeps all of us active. I'm excited to take him along on my hikes and trips this summer.

Exploring what BC has to offer

I've always enjoyed my hikes. They were a good way to stay fit and reward yourself with a nice view. This year, it became almost a passtime. With covid restrictions limiting what I could do and making it a struggle to stay in shape, I decided to make hiking a weekly thing. This summer I did a new hike almost every week and finshed the season by climbing the beautiful Panorama Ridge in Garibaldi Provincial Park.
